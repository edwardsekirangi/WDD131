About me

My name is Sekirangi Edward, I am from Uganda, often called the "Pearl of Africa". Uganda is a landlocked country in East Africa known for its stunning natural beauty and diverse wildlife. It is home to Lake Victoria, Africa's largest lake, and the source of the Nile River. Uganda is rich in culture, with over 50 ethnic groups, and offers incredible experiences like mountain gorilla trekking in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest. Its capital city is Kampala.
